# Donations

Automation Handbook is an independent project dedicated to creating **free and accessible knowledge about industrial automation**.

The goal of this handbook is to provide **clear, structured, and practical explanations** of automation technologies so that students, technicians, and engineers can learn without barriers.

Maintaining and expanding this project requires **time, infrastructure, and ongoing work**.  
If you find this resource valuable, consider supporting it through a donation.

---

# Why Donations Matter

Donations help support the development and maintenance of this project.

Your support helps cover:

- Website hosting and infrastructure
- Domain and platform costs
- Development of new tools and calculators
- Creation of new technical content
- Continuous updates and improvements

Even small contributions help keep the handbook **free and available to everyone**.

---

# Support the Project

If Automation Handbook has helped you learn something new, solve a problem, or better understand industrial automation, you can support the project with a donation.

Ways you may support the project include:

- One-time donations
- Recurring monthly support
- Sponsorships from individuals or companies

Your support directly contributes to the growth of this knowledge base.

---

# Transparency

Automation Handbook aims to remain:

- Independent
- Vendor-neutral
- Focused on educational value

Donations help ensure that the project can continue to grow **without restricting access to knowledge**.

---

# Other Ways to Support

If you cannot donate, you can still support the project by:

- Sharing the handbook with colleagues
- Contributing content
- Reporting errors or improvements
- Recommending topics to be added

You can learn more in **How to Contribute**.
