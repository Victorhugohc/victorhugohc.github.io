# Become a Partner

Automation Handbook is an independent educational project dedicated to providing **clear and accessible knowledge about industrial automation**.

The goal of this handbook is to help students, technicians, and engineers better understand the technologies used in modern industrial systems.

Companies and organizations can support this project by becoming **partners**.

Partnerships help sustain the development of the handbook while supporting the growth of accessible technical knowledge.

---

# Why Partner With Automation Handbook

Industrial automation is a rapidly growing field, and access to high-quality educational resources is essential.

By supporting this project, partners contribute to:

- Expanding accessible technical knowledge
- Supporting the next generation of automation engineers
- Promoting best practices in industrial automation
- Helping maintain a vendor-neutral learning resource

Partners also gain visibility within a community interested in **automation technologies and industrial systems**.

---

# Who Can Become a Partner

Automation Handbook welcomes partnerships with organizations involved in industrial technology, including:

- Industrial automation manufacturers
- System integrators
- Industrial distributors
- Engineering firms
- Technical training organizations
- Industrial software providers

Partnerships are intended to support **education and knowledge sharing within the automation industry**.

---

# Partnership Opportunities

Partnerships may include several forms of collaboration, such as:

## Project Sponsorship

Organizations may support the project through financial sponsorship that helps fund:

- Website infrastructure
- Development of engineering tools
- Creation of new educational content
- Platform improvements

Sponsors may be recognized on the website as supporters of the project.

---

## Technical Collaboration

Partners may collaborate by contributing:

- Technical knowledge
- Industry best practices
- Educational resources
- Example use cases

Technical collaboration helps ensure the handbook remains **relevant to real industrial environments**.

---

## Educational Support

Organizations that offer training programs, engineering courses, or certification programs may collaborate by supporting educational initiatives related to industrial automation.

---

# Vendor Neutrality

Automation Handbook is committed to maintaining **vendor-neutral educational content**.

Partnerships do not influence the technical explanations or recommendations presented in the handbook.

The goal of this project is to ensure that the content remains:

- Educational
- Objective
- Focused on engineering fundamentals
