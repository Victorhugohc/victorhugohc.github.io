# What is Automation Handbook

Automation Handbook is a structured knowledge base dedicated to **industrial automation, electrical engineering, and control systems**.

Its purpose is to provide **clear, practical, and vendor-neutral explanations** of the technologies used in modern industrial environments.

The handbook covers topics ranging from **basic electrical principles** to **advanced automation systems**, helping readers understand how industrial systems are designed, built, and maintained.

---

# The Goal of the Handbook

Industrial automation knowledge is often scattered across:

- Manufacturer manuals
- Training courses
- Technical standards
- Industry experience

Automation Handbook brings these concepts together into a **single organized resource** that focuses on understanding the **fundamental principles behind automation technologies**.

The goal is not to replace vendor documentation, but to provide the **conceptual foundation needed to understand and work with industrial systems**.

---

# What Makes This Handbook Different

Automation Handbook focuses on three core principles.

## Clear Explanations

Technical topics are explained in a way that is accessible to readers with different levels of experience.

Complex concepts are broken down into understandable sections so that both beginners and experienced professionals can benefit.

---

## Vendor-Neutral Knowledge

Many automation resources are tied to specific manufacturers.

This handbook focuses on **concepts that apply across different platforms and technologies**, helping readers understand how systems work regardless of the brand being used.

---

## Practical Understanding

Automation Handbook emphasizes **real-world engineering context**.

Instead of focusing only on theory, the content aims to explain how technologies are actually used in industrial systems.

Topics often include:

- how systems work
- where technologies are used
- key characteristics
- practical considerations

---

# What You Will Learn

The handbook covers a wide range of automation topics, including:

- Electrical fundamentals
- PLC systems and industrial control
- Industrial communication networks
- Sensors and actuators
- Drives and motion control
- Electrical panels and components
- Industrial safety
- Troubleshooting automation systems

These areas reflect the **core technologies used in modern factories and industrial processes**.

---

# Who Created This Handbook

Automation Handbook is an independent project created to make **industrial automation knowledge more accessible**.

The project grows through contributions from engineers, technicians, and professionals who want to share knowledge and improve technical education within the automation community.

---

# A Living Knowledge Base

Automation technology continues to evolve.

For this reason, Automation Handbook is designed to be a **living knowledge base** that can grow and improve over time.

Contributions, corrections, and suggestions help ensure the handbook remains:

- Accurate  
- Practical  
- Relevant to real industrial environments
